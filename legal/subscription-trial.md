# Subscription and 14-Day Trial Terms

**Last Updated:** 3 March 2026

These terms explain how Qualy trial and subscription billing work.

## 1. Trial Eligibility and Duration

- Qualy may offer a free trial period of 14 days.
- Trial may be limited to first-time accounts and can be changed, suspended, or ended at Qualy's discretion.

## 2. Credit Card Requirement During Trial

- Trial starts without mandatory credit card entry.
- Payment details are requested only when you choose to start a paid plan.

## 3. Transition to Paid Plan

- Paid service starts after plan selection and successful payment setup.
- If no paid plan is activated, trial access ends automatically.

## 4. Recurring Billing

- Paid subscriptions are billed on recurring cycles (monthly or yearly, based on selected plan).
- Renewal fees are charged automatically through the registered payment method.

## 5. Cancellation

- You may cancel recurring billing anytime before your next renewal date.
- Cancellation stops future renewals; already-paid periods remain active until period end.

## 6. Failed Payments

- If renewal charge fails, we may retry collection and notify you.
- Access may be downgraded or suspended if payment is not completed.

## 7. Price Changes

- Qualy may update subscription fees in the future.
- Material fee changes are notified before they take effect for renewal periods.
