# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Qualy Inc. ("Qualy", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares personal data when you use our website and services.

If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use the Service.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account and Organization Data

When you create or manage an account, we may collect:

- Name and contact details.
- Organization and billing details.
- Login and authentication information.

### 2.2 Workspace and Message Data

When you use Qualy, we may process:

- Conversations and message content.
- Customer metadata you upload or sync.
- Knowledge base content and operational settings.
- AI prompts, outputs, confidence signals, and lead-scoring data.

### 2.3 Technical and Usage Data

We may collect:

- Device/browser information.
- IP address and approximate location signals.
- Log events, error diagnostics, and usage analytics.

## 3. How We Use Data

We use personal data to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
- Authenticate users and secure accounts.
- Deliver AI-assisted features and customer support tools.
- Operate integrations (for example, messaging channels).
- Prevent abuse, fraud, and security incidents.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Legal Bases (Where Applicable)

Depending on jurisdiction, we process data under one or more legal bases:

- Performance of a contract.
- Legitimate interests.
- Legal obligations.
- Consent (where required).

## 5. How We Share Data

We may share data with:

- Service providers and subprocessors that help us operate the Service.
- Integration partners you enable (for example, messaging providers).
- Professional advisors and auditors under confidentiality duties.
- Authorities when required by law or valid legal process.

We do not sell personal data as that term is commonly used in major privacy laws.

## 6. International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries other than your own.

Where required, we use reasonable contractual and organizational safeguards for international transfers.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to:

- Provide the Service.
- Meet contractual commitments.
- Resolve disputes.
- Comply with legal obligations.

Retention periods may vary by data type and customer configuration.

## 8. Security

We use technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, and alteration.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

- Access your personal data.
- Correct inaccurate data.
- Delete certain data.
- Restrict or object to processing.
- Request portability.
- Withdraw consent where processing depends on consent.

To exercise rights, contact: **privacy@askqualy.com**.

## 10. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Keep sessions active.
- Remember preferences.
- Measure performance and usage.

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some Service functionality may be affected.

## 11. Children

The Service is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children where prohibited by law.

## 12. Third-Party Links and Services

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites or rely on third-party services.

Their data practices are governed by their own policies; this Privacy Policy does not cover third-party services.

## 13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make material changes, we will update the `last_updated` date and may provide additional notice where appropriate.
