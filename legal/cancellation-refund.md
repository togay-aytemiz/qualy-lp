# Cancellation, Refund, and Withdrawal Policy

**Last Updated:** 3 March 2026

This policy explains cancellation, withdrawal, and refund conditions for Qualy SaaS subscriptions.

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to paid subscription plans purchased online for Qualy digital services.

## 2. 14-Day Free Trial

- Qualy may provide a 14-day free trial without requiring credit card details.
- Trial access ends automatically at the end of trial period unless a paid subscription is activated.

## 3. Subscription Cancellation

- You may cancel recurring billing at any time from your account settings or by emailing support.
- Cancellation takes effect for upcoming billing cycles.
- Access to paid features continues until the end of the already-paid period.

## 4. Right of Withdrawal (Consumer)

- If you are a consumer, you may generally have 14 days to withdraw from a distance contract.
- However, for digital services started immediately with your explicit consent and your confirmation that you lose withdrawal right, the statutory withdrawal exception may apply.

## 5. Refund Rules

- Charges already collected for a started billing period are generally non-refundable.
- Refunds may be made when required by mandatory law, evident duplicate charging, or proven system-side payment errors.

## 6. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you believe a payment is unauthorized or incorrect, contact us first at askqualy@gmail.com so we can review and resolve the case quickly.

## 7. Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed through the payment provider and may take additional bank processing time depending on card issuer procedures.
