## 4. Legal Bases (KVKK Article 5)

Personal data is processed based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

- Explicit consent (Article 5/1).
- Necessity for the performance of a contract (Article 5/2-c).
- Compliance with legal obligations (Article 5/2-ç).
- Legitimate interests of the data controller, provided fundamental rights are not harmed (Article 5/2-f).

## 5. Transfer to Third Parties

Data may be transferred to:

- Infrastructure and technology vendors (hosting, database, analytics, messaging, AI providers).

## 6. International Data Transfers

Some service providers may process data outside Turkey (for example, AI processing and cloud infrastructure). Where required, international transfer mechanisms are implemented in accordance with KVKK Article 9.

## 7. Retention

Data is retained for the duration necessary for:

- Service delivery and account continuity.
- Contractual and legal obligations.
- Dispute resolution and audit requirements.

When retention is no longer required, data is deleted, destroyed, or anonymized in accordance with applicable law.

## 8. Data Subject Rights (KVKK Article 11)

You may request to:

- Learn whether your personal data is processed.
- Request information about processing.
- Learn the purpose of processing and whether data is used accordingly.
- Learn third parties to whom data is transferred.
- Request correction of incomplete or inaccurate data.
- Request deletion or destruction under legal conditions.
- Object to adverse outcomes resulting from automated processing.
- Claim compensation for unlawful processing.
